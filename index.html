Products

Fine Screen

Screen is the device used to retain solids found in the influent wastewater to the treatment plant. The main purpose of screening is to remove solid materials that could cause damage to other process equipment.

Screen Conveyer

The transport of the screenings using shaftless screw conveyors is becoming more widespread. The advantages of this system are numerous, if compared to the traditional screwshaft.

Grit Separtor

Grit separators are underground storage tanks with three chambers designed to remove heavy particulates, floating debris and hydrocarbons from stormwater.

Pre Treatment Stations

Combined pre-treatment station series 10 and 30 are specifically designed to treat flowup to 30m3/h. These solutions feature the same robustness and remarkable maintenance simplicity charachteristic of Fluiteco equipment.

Septic Tank Receipt

The Septic Tank Receipt unit is designed to pre-treat and receive septic tank effuent delivered by septage trucks. The goal is to treat the effiuent obtaining the maximum solid / liquid separation and ensure quick discharge time of the Septage trucks.

Sludge Treatment and Conveying

Sludge Thickner is an enclosed high performance filtration and dewatering system. It utillizers a central screw and an external filter element to gradually increase pressure to produce dry solids.
